About Us

At Gold Pearl Press, we believe books are as precious and unique as golden pearls.


Our team works together to find, polish and publish well-written, engaging novels with Christian values in a variety of genres, including sci-fi, fantasy, and historical and speculative fiction.


We do not rush this process. Our authors go through rounds of editing, proofreading, formatting and so on in an effort to add lustrous layers to their manuscripts, so their finished novels glow from within. Like pearls, we believe quality, enduring stories need time and attention to grow, and we hope ours will be cherished for years to come.
